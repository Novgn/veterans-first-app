## Session Overview

**Topic:** Veterans 1st Transportation - Multi-App Ecosystem
**Goals:** Generate comprehensive feature ideas across all business function applications

### Context Guidance

This session focuses on software and product development for a community-centered transportation service. Key exploration areas include:

- User problems and pain points for riders, drivers, and administrators
- Feature ideas across multiple application surfaces
- User experience flows prioritizing simplicity and accessibility
- Business model support through technology
- Differentiation from rideshare apps and medical transport providers

### Session Setup

**Target Audience:** Seniors, veterans, mobility-dependent residents in Raleigh-Durham area

**App Ecosystem Scope:**

- 📱 Rider App - booking, tracking, payments, communication
- 🚗 Driver App - trip management, navigation, earnings, availability
- 🖥️ Admin/Dispatch Console - scheduling, fleet management, customer service
- 💼 Business Operations - billing, reporting, compliance, partnerships

**Core Values to Reflect in Features:**

- Reliability and punctuality
- Transparent, predictable pricing
- Compassionate, dignified service
- Simplicity over complexity
- High-trust relationships

**Ethos:** "It's not about the miles. It's about the service."

## Technique Selection

**Approach:** AI-Recommended Techniques
**Analysis Context:** Multi-app ecosystem for veteran/senior transportation with accessibility-first focus

## Feature Inventory by App

### 📱 Rider App Features (28 total)

#### Booking & Scheduling

- One-tap recurring rides ("Every Tuesday 10am grocery" set once, done forever)
- Voice booking ("Hey app, book my doctor ride for Thursday")
- Photo destination (snap appointment card, AI extracts address)
- Family dashboard (daughter sees upcoming rides, gets pickup/dropoff alerts)
- Medication reminder integration ("Your dialysis ride is tomorrow. Did you take your meds?")
- 3-tap booking maximum (Where, when, confirm. Done.)
- Saved favorite destinations ("Home" "Harris Teeter" "Dr. Wilson")
- No account required (phone number is your ID)
- Confirmation call option ("Press 1 to confirm tomorrow's ride")
- MyChart/appointment sync (auto-suggest rides from medical calendar)
- Weather rescheduling ("Storm Thursday—move grocery to Wednesday?")
- Predictive booking ("It's Tuesday—usual grocery run?")

#### During the Ride

- Live ETA sharing (auto-text to doctor's office: "Margaret arriving in 8 mins")
- Driver photo, name, vehicle (big, clear, reassuring before pickup)
- Panic button (silent alert to dispatch + family contact)
- Comfort preferences saved ("Prefers front seat, no radio, AC at 72°")
- Live driver map (watch the car approach in real-time)
- Delivery windows ("Driver arriving between 9:45-10:00am")
- Proactive delay alerts ("Traffic on I-40. Dave will be 7 min late.")

#### Payments & Trust

- No-app payment option (monthly invoice mailed, pay by check)
- Price lock confirmation ("This trip is $45. No surprises. Ever.")
- Tip later (text link after ride to add tip)
- Loyalty program (10 rides = 1 free local trip)
- Tier status (Bronze/Silver/Gold riders get priority booking)
- Referral program ("Refer a neighbor, both get $10 credit")
- Annual summary ("You took 147 rides with us this year.")

#### Accessibility & UX

- MASSIVE touch targets (buttons you can't miss)
- High contrast mode (default, not buried in settings)
- Font scaling (text size that doesn't require reading glasses)
- Voice control throughout
- Physical NFC card option (tap card to call for ride—no app needed)
- Undo window ("Ride cancelled. Tap here within 60 seconds to restore.")
- Human escalation ("Having trouble? Press here to talk to someone")
